COVID-19 Message from Urban Ed

During these unprecedented times Urban Ed is still open for business and all classroom and online IT cert courses are continuing. Our work with middle school youth is also continuing through digital learning plans. Enrollment is still open and now is an excellent time to reskill or upskill for the ever growing demand for IT pros. We have shifted all physical classroom sessions to a live daily virtual delivery and still offer our mentored self-paced online courses covering many in-demand career pathways. Learn more about our STEMAcad and enroll today.

Thanks to Crown Castle, Urban Ed is also able to help some students receive a minimum wage stipend while they train with us. We truly appreciate their partnership during these very challenging times. Learn more about our partnership with Crown Castle through our Press Release!

ABOUT Us.

Headquartered in Washington, DC, Urban Ed, Inc. is a 501(c)(3) corporation whose mission is to provide District of Columbia children, youth and adults with technology-driven education, information and skill development for sustained futures.

Our COMMUNITY.

Lack of education and low performance early in children's educational paths lead to the multiple issues we face in our communities such as truancy, high dropout rates, high unemployment and ultimately perpetual poverty. Education and literacy is the root to these issues and Urban Ed's approach is to address current issues as well as root causes.
