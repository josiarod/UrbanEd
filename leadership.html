LEADERSHIP

MEET OUR LEADERSHIP TEAM
Our board of directors have begun the development of our new strategic direction for the 2019 fiscal years and beyond. In addition to the board of directors, Urban Ed is strongly supported and guided by an advisory committee of representatives from Fortune 500 corporations, small and mid-size businesses and educational institutions. Interested in becoming a member of the board? Contact President, Roxanne J. Williams at RWilliams@urbaned.org

Ashley Bratton

Board Chair
Ashley Bratton is an Associate at Promontory Financial Group (PFG), an IBM Company, where she helps clients implement process and technology changes to improve efficiency, reduce risk, and promote growth. Prior to PFG to worked as a teacher and coordinator in the Knowledge is Power Program network of charter schools in the District of Columbia. Additionally, she sits on the Board of the UNForgotten Fund, which provides microloans to women in Lusaka, Zambia. She holds a B.S. from Georgetown University and a M.P.A in social policy from Cornell University.

Nancy McGuire Choi

Board Vice Chair/Secretary
Nancy McGuire Choi is a social sector executive with 15 years of experience in technology innovation, information management, and international development. She is currently the Chief Operating Officer at Development Gateway, an international social enterprise that creates technologies to advance data-driven decisions for governments, development agencies, and civil society organizations in 60+ countries. Nancy received an MS in Foreign Service from the Georgetown University School of Foreign Service and a BA in Anthropology from Harvard University.

Darick Brown, Jr.

Board Member
Darick Brown, Jr. is a Help Desk Tech Tier II at Lintech Global, Inc. Darick is also an Urban Ed alumni! He is a new board member to Urban Ed and his complete bio is coming soon.

Azunna Anyanwu

Board Member
Azunna Anyanwu is a proven technology executive and servant leader who has held increasing levels of responsibility over his 18+ year career providing technology consulting services to Federal and State Government agencies. Azunna is adept at developing and sustaining trusted business advisor relationships with clients across IT and business units and his experience spans across systems implementation/integration, project management, technology strategy, and architecture domains. Azunna also possess significant experience with recruiting, mentoring and developing staff and has managed large cross-functional and multi-disciplinary teams. A native of DC, Azunna earned a BA in Computer Science from Harvard University and a MS in Computer Science from Johns Hopkins University. He also serves as an Adjunct Faculty in the GWU School of Engineering and Applied Science.

Joseph Costa

Board Member
Joe Costa is an Associate Vice President at The Cohen Group, a Washington, D.C. based global consulting and strategic advisory firm, where he leads Fortune 100 client relationships at the senior executive level. Joe provides strategic advice to corporate senior management on political and regulatory risk as well as go-to-market strategies, including market sizing, lead generation, customer targeting, and partnership development. He supports senior executives on the political, legal, and regulatory factors that may impact their company's ability to successfully enter and operate in a market. He also helps open and sustain lines of communication with government, business, non-profit, media and financial leaders who could impact a company's success in the market. Joe is a mentor a Dcode42, a start-up accelerator for technology firms. He is a Truman National Security Project Fellow and received a Masters in Public Policy from the University of Chicago where he was a McCormick Tribune Leadership Fellow in the Program for Urban and Community Leadership.

Keilyn Kirkland

Board Member
Keilyn Kirkland is an IT Security Director at General Dynamics IT. She is a new board member to Urban Ed and her complete bio is coming soon.

Jessica Martin

Board Member
Jessica Martin is Director, Demand Generation & Marketing Operations at Upskill, an enterprise augmented reality software startup. Prior to joining Upskill, she was Director, Global Demand Generation and Digital Marketing at Oracle where she led all campaign strategy, development and execution for the Energy and Utilities business unit. She joined Oracle through the acquisition of Opower in 2016. Her career in marketing has spanned both the professional services and tech industries. This experience sparked her interest and passion in bringing more diversity to the tech sector and making STEM education more accessible. Jessica graduated from the University of Maryland Smith School of Business with her MBA in 2013, and also received her BA in Communications from Elizabethtown College. She currently serves on the board of Urban Ed and is a member of the Marketing & Communications committee.

Sean McCue

Treasurer
Sean McCue is a Principal at Kearney & Company, a CPA firm based in Alexandria, VA that provides accounting, auditing, and information technology services to the Federal Government. He is responsible for business development and managing consulting engagements related to accounting, financial reporting, and audit remediation. In addition, he is active within Kearney’s mentorship programs, leading training sessions and working with entry and mid-level employees to develop their technical skills and continue to advance their careers. Sean is originally from Pennsylvania, and moved to the DC area immediately after graduating from the University of Pittsburgh in 2006. Although he has been in the DC area for more than 10 years, he is still a passionate Pittsburgh sports fan. Sean is a Certified Public Accountant and a Certified Government Financial Manager.
