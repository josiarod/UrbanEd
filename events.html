Sep 8, 2018 Career Breakthrough Day!
This Career Breakthrough half day was packed with hands-on career advancement workshops and mini bootcamps on cybersecurity, business development, software development, cloud technology and IT support. The event was hosted by the American Council for Technology and Industry Advisory Council (ACT-IAC) in partnership with Urban Ed to provide opportunities for Urban Ed students and alumni to gain new skills from experts in the field as well as network and find new professional mentors.

11th Annual Event & Graduation Ceremony
Urban Ed held a special graduation ceremony during our 11th Annual Event on October 3, 2011. The event was well attended and marked another major success for Urban Ed's program delivery. Our STEMACAD was launched in Feb 2011 and we have developed an approach to help the unemployed gain four global technology certifications and secure entry-level jobs earning at least $17-$19 per hour. Our success shows much promise for the future of our IT training and for helping the unemployed gain sustainable employment, breaking the cycle of poverty!
